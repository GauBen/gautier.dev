<h1>Self-Hosted</h1>

This website is served from my living room!

At the beggining of 2026 I took the resolution to **take back ownership of my data**. To that end, I bought a €150 mini PC ([Soyo M4 Plus 2, Intel N150, 16 GB RAM, 512 GB SSD](https://fr.aliexpress.com/w/wholesale-soyo-m4-plus-2.html)) with the plan of turning it into a low-power server.

It now runs [Proxmox VE](https://www.proxmox.com/en/products/proxmox-virtual-environment/overview). Here is my progress so far:

- [x] Personal website ([gautier.dev](https://gautier.dev))

  Deployment is fully automated: pushing to my own [Forgejo](https://forgejo.org/) instance ([git.gautier.dev](https://git.gautier.dev/gautier/gautier.dev)) triggers a webhook on my [Coolify](https://coolify.io/) instance.
